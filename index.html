What Is TinkerTaps

Screen time that
supports your child,
not just occupies them.

TinkerTaps gives parents a mobile app of thoughtfully designed sensory and hand-eye coordination activities they can take anywhere — the grocery store, grandma's house, a long car ride.

Most toddler apps are built around engagement metrics. TinkerTaps is built around child development. Every parent who's handed their kid a phone knows the difference when they feel it.

We're a family of four — not a big tech company. No ads, no overstimulation, no loud design choices made to maximize time-on-screen. Just something we'd actually hand our own kids.
