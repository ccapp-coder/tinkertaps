Activities & Features

TinkerTaps includes sensory activities and hand-eye coordination games — things like shape matching, color exploration, nature scenes, and interactive elements designed to engage babies and toddlers without overstimulating them. Every activity is calm, focused, and built around real developmental milestones.

The free version includes 2 activities. Subscribe to TinkerTaps Premium to unlock 5 additional activities for a total of 7, plus access to all future activities as they're added. We're always building more.

The Parental Lock is a built-in feature that keeps your child in the app once you've handed them the phone. No accidental exits, no navigating away, no wiping your device — a real concern any parent who's handed a toddler their phone knows too well. Tap the lock icon to enable it before handing the device over.

TinkerTaps includes gentle, calm audio that complements each activity. You can toggle sound on or off at any time using the sound icon in the app — perfect for waiting rooms, airplanes, or anywhere you need quiet play.

Subscription

TinkerTaps Premium ($3.99/month) unlocks five additional activity slides, access to all future activity expansions as they're released, and the drawing activity that lets your child create their own artwork. Screenshot that artwork and order a one-of-a-kind custom t-shirt from our Etsy shop.

Open TinkerTaps on your device, tap the More Activities button on the home screen, and follow the prompts to subscribe. Subscriptions are processed through Apple or Google — your payment stays with the platform you trust.

Subscriptions are managed through Apple (iOS) or Google (Android). To cancel on iOS: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → TinkerTaps → Cancel. On Android: Google Play → Menu → Subscriptions → TinkerTaps → Cancel. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

Tap More Activities on the home screen, then tap Refresh Purchases at the bottom of the screen. This syncs your subscription status. If activities still don't appear, close the app completely and reopen it. Still stuck? Email us at info@courtrightco.com.
